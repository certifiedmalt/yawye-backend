Ad Creative Tips for YAWYE

  • Hook Formula (first 3 seconds): "Do you REALLY know what's in your food?" + show the unhealthy 3/10 score image
  • Problem → Solution: Use the comparison image or pair unhealthy/healthy result screenshots
  • Best performing ad format: 15-30 second vertical video (combine 2-3 Sora clips with text overlays)
  • CTA: "Download free on Google Play" — always end with a clear call to action
  • Target audiences: Health-conscious parents, fitness enthusiasts, clean eating communities, people following anti-UPF influencers
  • A/B test: Try the "SCAN. SCORE. KNOW." story ad vs the lifestyle shopping photo
  • Platforms: Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts for video; Facebook/Instagram feed for image ads
