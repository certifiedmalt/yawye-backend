What the app does

You Are What You Eat is built to be simple enough for the supermarket aisle, but smart enough to help you gradually understand ultra‑processed foods (UPFs) and ingredients over time.

Scan

Barcode scanner + manual entry

Point your camera at any food barcode, or enter the code manually. The app pulls product data, ingredients and images, then runs an AI‑powered analysis focused on ultra‑processed ingredients.

Results

Health score & ingredient breakdown

See a Health Score (1–10) with a coloured ring, plus harmful vs beneficial ingredients explained in plain English. High‑risk additives and ultra‑processed markers are flagged clearly.

Habits

Streaks, XP, quests & badges

Build healthier habits with daily scan streaks, XP and levels, daily quests (“Scan 3 products”, “Find a healthy product”), and unlockable badges. The app nudges you with gentle reminders so you don’t break your streak.

Learn

Daily Food Quiz & AI assistant

Answer one short nutrition question per day and get XP and a simple explanation. Ask the AI health education assistant general questions about ingredients, labels and ultra‑processed foods.
