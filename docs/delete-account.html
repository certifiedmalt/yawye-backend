Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to request deletion of your You Are What You Eat account and all associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account, please send an email to:

youarewhatyoueat.sup@aol.com

Subject line: Account Deletion Request

Include: The email address associated with your account

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account information: Email address, name, password
  • Scan history: All products you have scanned
  • Favorites: Your saved favorite products
  • Progress data: XP, streaks, achievements, quiz history
  • Preferences: App settings and preferences

Data Retention Period

After receiving your deletion request:

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately
  • All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete

Data We May Retain

For legal and operational purposes, we may retain:

  • Transaction records: If you made any purchases (required for tax/legal compliance)
  • Anonymized analytics: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics

Alternative: Delete Data Without Deleting Account

If you want to clear your scan history but keep your account, you can do this within the app:

  • Go to Profile → Settings → Clear Scan History

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion, please contact us:

Email: youarewhatyoueat.sup@aol.com
